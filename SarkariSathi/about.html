üí° Why This Hybrid Approach?

Security & Authenticity: Government documents like passports, citizenship certificates, and licenses require physical verification, biometric data, and secure handling. Officers must verify original documents and identity in person.

Legal Requirements: Many government processes require face-to-face verification and physical signatures as per Nepal's legal framework.

Digital Convenience: While submission is offline, the platform saves citizens time by letting them prepare, book slots, and track progress online - eliminating multiple visits and long waits.
